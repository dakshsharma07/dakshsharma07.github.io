The Challenge

Agencies face significant challenges in managing and tracking the submission of required forms and reports from counties and other jurisdictions. Traditional methods often involve manual tracking, email reminders, and spreadsheets, which can lead to missed deadlines, incomplete submissions, and a lack of transparency in the process.

The current system lacks automation, real-time tracking, and centralized management capabilities, making it difficult for agencies to ensure compliance and maintain oversight over the reporting process.

The Solution

Civisight streamlines the form submission process with a centralized platform that automates reminders, tracks submissions in real-time, and provides comprehensive reporting capabilities. Our solution offers:

  • Automated Reminders: Never miss a deadline with our smart reminder system that keeps all stakeholders informed and on schedule.
  • Real-time Tracking: Monitor the status of all form submissions in one place, with instant updates and notifications.
  • Centralized Management: Manage all form submissions from a single, intuitive interface that provides complete visibility and control.
